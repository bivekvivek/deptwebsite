Training & Testimonials

How do we Train & make our students employable?

We rightly identify the Skill gap of the students by various analysis & assessments and equip them with proper training to meet the expectations of the prospective employers & industry standards.

We consider every student of us as a unique talent. We support and help our students in all the possible ways so that they get placed in a Good company which will give them a bright career opportuniti

  • We provide them the perspectives of Soft Skills, Verbal and Aptitude Skills with the blend of Skills in Recent Technologies.
  • We perform various levels of assessments & Map the skill level of the students to spot the gap between the industrial expectations and the current skill level of the students as Training Needs.
  • We Develop a full-fledged and need based Training Plan that meets all the different skill levels of our students.
  • We do roll out essential training programs on various domains such as Soft Skills, Verbal Ability and Aptitude Skills.
  • Evaluating effectiveness of the Training programs by analyzing the feedback obtained from the trainees and implementing the necessary corrective actions in order to enhance the effectiveness of the Training programs
